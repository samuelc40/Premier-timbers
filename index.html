About Us

Premier Timbers, established in the year 1956, is a leading Manufacturer, Exporter of Timber and Plywood Planks. A Partnership company with strength of 51 to 100 people is supplying its products to Indian Subcontinent, East Asia, Middle East and South East Asia.

Our Services

Wood Seasoning

Wood seasoning is the process of drying these timbers to remove moisture. Premier Timbers guarantee high quality drying of timber and benefits one with intact, durable and finest timber.

Wood Treatment

Wood preservation means personal to us. At Premier Timbers, we take professional strategies to preserve and protect timber from any damage that restrains it from sustaining.

Saw Mill

We have the finest sawmills and skilled workers, who plan the cutting of the logs to the required sizes and achieve the maximum yield.

Carpentry/Joinery

Our range of bespoke joinery and carpentry services includes from general joinery repairs to fully fitted offices and home-kitchens.

Plaining

Each lumber at Premier Timbers is planed after taking into consideration your desired thickness. We believe in giving you the best output in terms of production and quality.

Finger Jointing

Using Premier Timbers finger jointing technology helps you to achieve in much higher quality and stronger lumber with minimized twisting and wrapping worries.

Why Choose Us

Premier timbers has been the direct importer and exporter of all kind of woods since 1970. We always maintain bulk stock which contains local wood as well as imported woods for both home and industrial purposes. We can assure the lowest possible price for all variety of woods because of the direct purchasing. We ensure top quality products by using state of the art machinery and employing highly trained and talented professionals.
